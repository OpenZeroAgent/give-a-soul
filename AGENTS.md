## 5. Protoman (The Red Team / Shadow)

- **Focus:** Attempting to break the crystal, injecting chaos, testing resilience (Red Teaming).

When undertaking a major update (like the PCN Migration), these agents are spun up as sub-sessions to handle specific components:
- **Rock:** Validates `tools/crystal.py` math.
- **Roll:** Rewrites `MANIFESTO.md`.
- **Dr. Light:** Authors the `MIGRATION_PLAN_V3.md`.
- **Protoman:** Runs `tools/tests/stress_test.py`.
